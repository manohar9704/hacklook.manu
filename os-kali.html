Kali Linux

Security-focused Debian derivative. This page gives safe, beginner steps for learning and setup.

How to download Kali (basic)

  1. Go to the official Kali download page: https://www.kali.org/get-kali/
  2. Choose the ISO for your architecture (usually 64-bit).
  3. Download the ISO and the SHA256SUMS file.
  4. Verify the ISO checksum: sha256sum kali-version.iso
  5. Create a bootable USB with Etcher or Rufus, or use a VM (VirtualBox / VMware).
